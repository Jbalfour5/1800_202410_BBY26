About Us

UrbanEx is a school project proudly developed by the BBY-26 team: Valley, Haider, and Irene. As passionate students, we combined our skills and creativity to build a platform that enhances community safety and connectivity. UrbanEx empowers users to explore urban areas with confidence, featuring real-time updates, community-driven posts, and safety reports. Every aspect of the app reflects our dedication to fostering safer and more informed neighborhoods. This project represents not just our technical abilities but also our shared vision for using technology to create a positive impact. UrbanEx is more than a class project—it’s our contribution to building stronger, more connected communities.
